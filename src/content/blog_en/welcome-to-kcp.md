## What is KCP?

KCP Co., Ltd. is a business process outsourcing (BPO) company built for the AI era. We combine the speed of AI agents with human judgment to deliver high-quality results at a fraction of traditional costs.

## Why We Started

Modern businesses are drowning in operational work — social media management, content creation, development coordination, and more. These tasks are necessary, but they pull founders and teams away from their core work.

We believe AI can change that. By combining tools like Claude Code with experienced human oversight, we can handle these operations efficiently and reliably.

## What We Do

- **Social Media Management** — AI-assisted scheduling, analysis, and engagement
- **Web & App Development** — Full-stack builds and project direction
- **AI Training** — Practical workshops to help your team adopt AI tools
- **Media Operations** — Content creation and blog management

## Our Approach

We keep things simple, small, and fast. Every task gets broken down to its simplest form, automated where possible, and completed quickly. This isn't about replacing people — it's about giving people back their time.
